Designing a Toaster

User research and design ideation

Project Overview

In this design project, I conducted extensive primary and secondary research to understand user needs and market trends. I developed designs and ideated potential prototypes, but did not progress to the prototype phase.

Role and Contributions

Led research efforts, developed design concepts, and ideated potential prototypes.

Key Skills and Tools Used

  • Primary and Seconday Research
  • Conceptual Design
  • Ideation and Sketching
  • User Needs Analysis

Design Process

Research

Conducted thorough primary and secondary research, including user interviews and market analysis, to inform design decisions.

Ideation

Developed multiple design concepts through brainstorming sessions and sketching, focusing on user needs and market trends.

Conceptual Design

Refined selected concepts into detailed design proposals, ready for prototyping.

Conclusion

Challenges and Solutions

Addressed various design challenges by iterating on concepts and incorporating user feedback, ensuring the designs met identified needs.

Final Outcome

Comprehensive design proposal for a toaster, highlighting extensive research and ideation skills, ready for future prototyping.

Impact and Learnings

Gained valuable experience in research, ideation, and conceptual design, emphasizing the importance of understanding user needs and market trends.
