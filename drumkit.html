Modeling a Drum Kit

Using SolidWorks to model a full drum kit assembly

Project Overview

Modeled a drum kit in SolidWorks, showcasing advanced CAD skills and comprehensive analysis techniques as part of Brown University's Computer Aided Design and Visualization course.

Role and Contributions

Collaborated in a group to measure a physical drum kit, model parts, create components, and assemble the full kit in SolidWorks. Was responsible for the completion of the stool.

Key Skills and Tools Used

  • CAD Modeling(SolidWorks)
  • CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing)
  • Motion Analysis
  • Finite Element Analysis(FEA)

Design Process

Measurement and Modeling

Measured a physical drum kit and modeled individual parts in SolidWorks.

Component Creation and Assembly

Created components from parts and assembled the entire drum kit, ensuring accurate representation and functionality.

Analysis

Performed CAM, motion analysis, and finite element analysis to evaluate the model's manufacturing feasability, movement, and structural integrity.

Conclusion

Challenges and Solutions

Simplified the model due to time contraints while maintaining essential details, demonstrating adaptability and efficient time management.

Final Outcome

A detailed and accurate SolidWorks model of a drum kit, complete with analysis reports and visualization, highlighting proficiency in advanced CAD techniques and analysis.

Impact and Learnings

Enhanced my CAD modeling skills and learned to integrate various tools with SolidWorks, preparing me for complex design projects.
