Modeling a Portable Straw

Using SolidWorks to model a portable straw and case

Project Overview

Modeled a portable straw and case, demonstrating detailed CAD work and technical documentation skills.

Role and Contributions

Individually modeled the parts, designed the case, created the assembly, and produced comprehensive engineering drawings.

Key Skills and Tools Used

  • CAD Modeling(SolidWorks)
  • Assembly Creation
  • Engineering Drawings

Design Process

Modeling

Modeled each part of the portable straw and its case in SolidWorks, ensuring precision and functionality.

Assembly Creation

Assembled the modeled parts into a complete, functional portable straw, verifying fit and design integrity.

Engineering Drawings

Produced detailed engineering drawings for all parts and the final assembly, demonstrating technical documentation proficiency.

Conclusion

Challenges and Solutions

Overcame complex component interactions by refining the design iteratively, ensuring a functional and practical final product.

Final Outcome

A meticulously modeled and assembled portable straw with comprehensive engineering drawings, highlighting advanced CAD skills and attention to detail.

Impact and Learnings

Refined my CAD modeling and technical documentation skills, gaining valuable experience in creating detailed and functional product designs.
