Modeling a Tumbler

Using SolidWorks to model a coffee tumbler

Project Overview

In my first CAD modeling project, I chose to model a coffee tumbler with three components, demonstrating my ability to handle simpler yet precise CAD tasks and produce accurate technical documentation.

Role and Contributions

Individually modeled the parts, created the assembly, and produced comprehensive engineering drawings.

Key Skills and Tools Used

  • CAD Modeling(SolidWorks)
  • Assembly Creation
  • Engineering Drawings

Design Process

Modeling

Modeled each part of the coffee tumbler in SolidWorks, focusing on precision and simplicity.

Assembly Creation

Assembled the modeled parts into a complete coffee tumbler, ensuring accurate fit and functionality.

Engineering Drawings

Produced detailed engineering drawings for all parts and the final assembly, showcasing my technical documentation skills.

Conclusion

Challenges and Solutions

Simplified the design process by focusing on essential components, ensuring functionality within the project's scope.

Final Outcome

A precisely modeled and assembled coffee tumbler with comprehensive engineering drawings, highlighting foundational CAD skills and attention to detail.

Impact and Learnings

Developed fundamental CAD modeling and technical documentation skills, providing a solid foundation for more complex projects.
